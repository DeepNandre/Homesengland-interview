Methodology

This dashboard combines data from multiple authoritative sources to provide a comprehensive view of housing trends and opportunities:

  • Office for National Statistics housing data
  • Land Registry price paid data
  • Homes England investment and outcome data
  • Local authority planning and housing delivery data

Advanced analytics techniques applied include:

  • Time series forecasting for housing delivery projections
  • Geospatial analysis for opportunity identification
  • Correlation analysis between investment and outcomes
  • Scenario modeling for policy impact assessment

About This Project

This interactive dashboard was developed to demonstrate how data analytics can support Homes England's mission to accelerate housing delivery and improve affordability across England.

By transforming complex housing data into accessible insights, this tool enables strategic decision-making at all levels of the organization.

The dashboard is designed to be:

  • Accessible - Usable by both technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Actionable - Providing clear insights that can inform policy and investment decisions
  • Adaptable - Capable of incorporating new data sources as they become available
  • Accurate - Built on reliable data with transparent methodology
